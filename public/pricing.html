FAQ

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

The Free plan is available forever. You can try all Pro features with a 14-day free trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), WeChat Pay, and Alipay.

How does the phone call alert work?

When an urgent account tweets, we send a Feishu message and trigger a phone call via Feishu. If you don't read the message, we retry automatically.
